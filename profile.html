FOREST INVESTMENT PROGRAM - III

The Forest Investment Program 1 (FIP-1) administered by the Asian Development Bank (ADB) seeks to support the Indonesian government and indigenous peoples in sustainable forest management and reducing greenhouse gas emission (GHG). The Community-Focused Investments to Address Deforestation and Forest Degradation project supports capacity and livelihood building of local communities around the forest and is expected to provide environmental and livelihood benefits for the community. Furthermore, an expected outcome is to advance the implementation of REDD+ in the area surrounding the program in West Kalimantan Province. The project is also expected to contribute to the effectiveness of REDD+ fiscal strategy and harmonization at the provincial and national levels.

In the context of the implementation of this FIP-1 program, PT Hatfield Indonesia (PTHI) manages the Project Implementation Support Unit (PISU) based on contract number K.01 / BUPSHA-3 / PPK / FIP1 / 2017 between PTHI with the Ministry of Environment and Forestry (MoEF), Directorate General of Social
